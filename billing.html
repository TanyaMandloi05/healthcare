Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my insurance information?

You can update your insurance information by logging into your account and navigating to the Insurance section under Billing. If you need assistance, please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567.

What if I can't pay my bill in full?

We offer flexible payment plans for patients who need assistance. Please contact our billing department to discuss your options and set up a payment plan that works for you.

How do I dispute a charge on my bill?

If you believe there is an error on your bill, please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 or email billing@healthcareplus.com. We'll review your account and resolve any discrepancies.

When will my insurance be billed?

We typically submit claims to insurance companies within 2-3 business days after your appointment. Processing times vary by insurance provider, but most claims are processed within 30 days.
